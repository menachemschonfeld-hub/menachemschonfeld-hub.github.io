— What we believe

How we work, and why.

Six principles that determine how we quote, how we advise, and how we handle a claim. They're written down because we've seen what happens when a brokerage doesn't have any.

01

Independent. Always.

We don't work for a single carrier. We work for you. That means we shop coverage across 25+ A-rated markets every quote, every renewal — and recommend whichever combination of price, terms, and carrier fit best.

Captive agents represent one company. Direct writers cut out brokers but also cut out advice. We sit between the two — independent enough to recommend any carrier, professional enough to actually advise.

02

Coverage first. Price second.

The wrong policy at the right price is still the wrong policy. We start with what needs to be covered — actual exposures, real limits, the endorsements that matter — and only then look at where to place it competitively.

If you ask for cheaper, we'll tell you what cheaper costs you. Sometimes the answer is "yes, that's a fine tradeoff." Sometimes it's "absolutely not — here's why."

03

Stay during the claim.

Most agents disappear when the loss happens. Adjuster shows up, you're on your own. We don't operate that way.

When you file a claim with SafeSide, your producer is on the file. We work the carrier on your behalf, push for the right adjuster, escalate when something stalls, and stay until the claim is resolved — or we tell you why it can't be.

04

Read every line.

Most policies are bound without anyone reading them carefully. We're not most brokers. We compare quotes line-by-line — endorsements, exclusions, sub-limits, definitions — and tell you exactly where each carrier's offer differs.

The clause buried on page 47 is often the clause that matters most when something goes wrong.

05

Plain language only.

The insurance industry uses jargon as a moat — it keeps you confused enough to not ask the right questions. We refuse to operate that way.

If we use a term, we'll define it. If a coverage is complicated, we'll diagram it. If a policy doesn't make sense to you, that's our failure to explain — not your failure to understand.

06

Long relationships, not transactions.

The average client relationship at SafeSide is over twelve years. The average across the industry is closer to three. We earn that by being the broker you don't have to think about until you do — and being honest, available, and responsive when you do.

Our retention rate isn't a marketing stat. It's the only metric that actually matters in this business.
