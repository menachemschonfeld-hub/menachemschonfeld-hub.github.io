— Our Story

A family practice in an industry that forgot how.

SafeSide Insurance was founded in 2007 with a clear conviction: that insurance had drifted away from its purpose. Policies got longer. Carriers got bigger. Brokers got further from the people they served. Claims went from a phone call to a portal ticket to a denial letter.

We wanted to be different. Not by selling more — but by selling less, more carefully. By reading every policy before we recommend it. By picking up the phone when something goes wrong. By treating our clients the way we'd want to be treated when our own families are at stake.

"Insurance is a promise. Most brokers sell it. We keep it."

Today, SafeSide protects thousands of families and hundreds of businesses across New York, New Jersey, Florida, and beyond. We work with 25+ top-rated carriers and place over $500 million in coverage annually. But we still answer the phone. And our name is still on the door.

— What We Stand For

Four convictions. Always.

Independence

We work for you, not the carriers. Our recommendations come from your interests — never from sales quotas or carrier incentives.

Honesty

If you don't need a policy, we'll say so. If a coverage gap concerns us, we'll flag it. Even when it costs us a sale.

Advocacy

When the claim happens, we're your representative — not the carrier's processor. We fight for what your policy promises.

Continuity

Your producer answers your call ten years from now. No churn, no handoffs, no "your account manager has changed."
