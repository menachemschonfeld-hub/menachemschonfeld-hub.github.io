— Overview

A second layer when one isn't enough.

Personal umbrella sits on top of your other policies — homeowners, watercraft, secondary residences — and adds an extra $1M, $5M, or $10M of liability protection. It's the cheapest insurance dollar-for-dollar you'll ever buy. And in our experience, the one most clients wish they'd added before the lawsuit.
