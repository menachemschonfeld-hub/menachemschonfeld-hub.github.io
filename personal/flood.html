— Overview

Flood is the gap most homeowners learn about too late.

Standard homeowners insurance excludes flood damage. Period. And about a quarter of NFIP flood claims come from properties outside high-risk flood zones. If you're near water, in a low-lying area, or even just in a place where heavy rain pools — you should at least consider flood coverage. We write through both the NFIP (federal) and private markets.
