— Overview

Standard policies fall short on the things that matter.

Most homeowners policies cap jewelry at $1,500–$2,500 total — total. A single ring above that limit means the rest is uninsured. Scheduled personal property (a 'rider' or 'floater') covers each item at its appraised value with broader peril coverage, including mysterious disappearance, breakage, and travel.
