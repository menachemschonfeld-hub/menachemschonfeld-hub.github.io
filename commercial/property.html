Protecting the physical foundation of your business.

Whether you own the building, lease it, or run a multi-property portfolio, your physical assets — building, contents, inventory, equipment — need protection. We write commercial property as a standalone policy or as part of a package, with replacement-cost valuation, broad-form perils, and ordinance-and-law endorsements that fill the gaps that bite at claim time.

— Frequently Asked

Common questions.

Actual cash value pays depreciated value at claim time — often 30–60% less than replacement. Replacement cost pays what it actually costs to rebuild or replace, with no depreciation. We almost always recommend replacement cost.

Co-insurance penalties apply when you under-insure your property. If your building is worth $1M and you insure it for $700K under an 80% co-insurance clause, you'll be penalized at every claim. We audit this at every renewal.

No — flood is excluded under standard commercial property. We write flood through NFIP and private markets separately.

After a major loss, building codes may require you to upgrade — handicap accessibility, sprinkler systems, electrical. Ordinance & law covers the additional cost to bring damaged property to current code.
