— Overview

Required by law. Critical to your team.

Workers' compensation is mandated in nearly every state for businesses with employees. It pays medical care and wage replacement when an employee is injured on the job — and protects you from being sued by the injured employee. We place WC across all 50 states, including the harder-to-write trades (roofing, demolition, restaurants, healthcare).
