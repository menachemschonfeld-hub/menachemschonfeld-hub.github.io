— Commercial · D&O

Personal protection for leadership.

Directors & Officers (D&O) liability insurance protecting executives, board members, and the entity from management-decision lawsuits.

— Overview

Leadership decisions create personal exposure.

D&O protects the personal assets of directors, officers, and (in many policies) the entity itself when management decisions trigger lawsuits. Shareholder suits, regulatory investigations, employment claims (often via EPL endorsement), breach-of-fiduciary-duty allegations. Critical for any company with a board, outside investors, or employees — and increasingly required by VCs, lenders, and partners.
