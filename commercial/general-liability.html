— Overview

The lawsuit-defense layer every business needs.

General Liability is the foundational lawsuit-defense coverage every business needs. It pays defense costs (which are often the largest expense) and damages if your business is sued for bodily injury, property damage, personal injury, or advertising injury. Without GL, a single lawsuit can take down a profitable company — even if the suit is meritless.
