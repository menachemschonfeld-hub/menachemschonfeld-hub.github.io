— Overview

Real cyber coverage — not the BOP endorsement.

The cyber coverage tucked into your BOP — usually $25K or $50K — won't cover a real breach. A ransomware event, a wire-fraud incident, a stolen laptop with PII — these regularly cost $250K to $2M+. Standalone cyber covers the breach response (forensics, legal, notification, credit monitoring), the extortion payment, the business interruption, and the regulatory fines.
