— Privacy Policy

How we handle your information.

Effective date: January 1, 2026

SafeSide Insurance ("SafeSide," "we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect personal information when you visit safesideins.com or work with us as a client.

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly when requesting a quote, filing a claim, or contacting us — including your name, address, phone, email, business information, and details necessary to evaluate insurance needs (property information, vehicle information, employee counts, prior claim history, etc.).

For active clients, we also receive information from our carrier partners — policy details, billing status, claim activity, and underwriting communications.

We collect limited automatically-gathered information about your visit to safesideins.com — IP address, device type, browser, pages visited, and referring URL — through standard analytics.

How we use your information

  • To prepare quotes and submit applications to insurance carriers on your behalf.
  • To service your existing policies — renewals, endorsements, claims, and certificates of insurance.
  • To communicate with you about your insurance, our services, and relevant industry updates.
  • To comply with legal, regulatory, and licensing obligations.
  • To improve our website and services.

How we share your information

We share your information only as needed to deliver our services or as required by law. Specifically:

  • With insurance carriers — to obtain quotes, bind coverage, and service policies.
  • With premium financing companies — only if you elect to finance your premium.
  • With service providers — IT, agency-management software, and similar vendors who process information on our behalf under written confidentiality agreements.
  • As required by law — court orders, subpoenas, regulatory inquiries, or law-enforcement requests.

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your information for third-party advertising purposes.

Cookies and tracking

safesideins.com uses limited cookies for site functionality and analytics. We do not use cross-site tracking or third-party advertising cookies.

Security

We maintain reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure — but we treat client data with the care it deserves and limit access internally to those who need it.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights regarding your personal information — including the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your information. Contact privacy@safesideins.com for any privacy-related request.

Children's privacy

safesideins.com is not directed to children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Effective date" above reflects the most recent revision. We encourage you to check back periodically.
