Our commitment

SafeSide Insurance is committed to providing a website that is accessible to the widest possible audience, regardless of technology or ability. We aim to comply with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA.

Standards

Our internal goals include:

  • Semantic HTML structure with proper heading hierarchy.
  • Sufficient color contrast for all text.
  • Keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements.
  • Descriptive alt text for meaningful images.
  • Form labels and ARIA attributes where appropriate.
  • Reasonable text resizing without loss of function.

Known limitations

Some embedded third-party content (maps, payment portals) may not fully comply with WCAG standards. We work to provide alternative methods of access — phone, email — for any function that may not be fully accessible.

Equal access to insurance

Independent of website accessibility, SafeSide is committed to serving clients regardless of disability, age, or technology proficiency. Our team is happy to walk through coverage options by phone, in person at our Spring Valley office, or by mailed correspondence — whatever works for you.
